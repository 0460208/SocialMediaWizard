How We Got Our Start

In 2019, after a decade of customer service experience in restaurants, Amanda Martel decided to enroll in Clark State College. She began an education in New Media where she discovered a love for the internet and social media. After graduation, Amanda worked as a social media content creator where she developed her knowledge and skillset.

In 2022, Social Media Wizard was born! Through her education Amanda had discovered a passion for social media and the impact it can have on small to medium sized businesses. She plans to help companies utilize social media as a tool to engage with clients and build a better brand online. Amanda has taken her education, passion, and experience to bring together a bundle of services to help your company flourish.

Our Mission

At Social Media Wizard, our mission is to bring trustworthy, dependable social media services to small and medium sized businesses (SMBs). We work hard to build custom solutions that help companies succeed online. We are dedicated to high-quality content, 100% client satisfaction, and affordable pricing. Our team is working towards a better social media world for SMBs. Together, we can create a stronger online presence that boosts revenue, promotes branding, and encourages engagement.
